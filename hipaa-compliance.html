HIPAA Compliance Checklist

Privacy Rule

  • Protected Health Information (PHI): Ensure PHI is protected and handled according to HIPAA regulations.
  • Use and Disclosure: Limit use and disclosure of PHI to the minimum necessary to achieve the intended purpose.

Security Rule

  • Administrative Safeguards: Implement policies and procedures for security measures.
  • Physical Safeguards: Protect physical access to electronic information systems and facilities.
  • Technical Safeguards: Use technology to protect electronic PHI (ePHI).

Breach Notification Rule

  • Notification to Individuals: Notify affected individuals within 60 days of discovering a breach.
  • Notification to HHS: Report breaches affecting 500 or more individuals to HHS immediately.
  • Notification to Media: Notify prominent media outlets if the breach affects more than 500 residents of a state or jurisdiction.

Training and Awareness

  • Employee Training: Provide regular training on HIPAA compliance and data protection.
  • Ongoing Awareness: Keep staff informed about changes to HIPAA regulations and best practices.

Documentation and Record Keeping

  • Policies and Procedures: Maintain up-to-date policies and procedures related to HIPAA compliance.
  • Records of Compliance: Document all compliance efforts, including training, risk assessments, and breach notifications.
